Palpak! Ito ang patutsada kahapon ni Sen. Miriam Defensor-Santiago kay dating Special Action Force (SAF) commander Director Getulio Napenas nang pangunahan nito ang operasyon sa Mamasapano, Maguindanao na ikinasawi ng 44 commandos. "You're a failure. You're incompetent. You cited intervening factors. The operation should have proceeded smoothly eh kayo nagpaplano," sambit ni Santiago. Bago ito, sinabi ni Napenas na hindi nakara-ting sa oras ang assault force o 84th SAF company sa target -- sa kubo ni Malaysian terrorist Malaysian terrorist Zulkifli bin Hir, alyas Marwan. "Bakit hindi kayo pumunta ng maaga pa para hindi pa gising mga Muslim? Alam niyong 5 a. m. ang prayer nila," sabi ni Santiago. "Ang problema sa 'yo, you're assuming full responsibility for the operation. I doubt very much your statement that you're in full sole control," anang senadora. "Maraming mali sa timing, sa execution (The timing and the execution are flawed) that's why you shouldn't be blamed for the whole fiasco," wika pa nito.
