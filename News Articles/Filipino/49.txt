Todo-tanggi si Jasmine Curtis-Smith sa tsika na siya talaga ang paborito ng TV5 kaya siya lang ang parating may project sa mga female stars ng naturang network.Sey ni Jasmine, wala raw gano’n sa Kapatid Network dahil pantay-pantay ang trato sa kanilang lahat. Nagkataon lang na meron siyang weekly show na Move It: Clash of the Street Dancers at ang magsisimula ng Wattpad Presents A House Full of Hunks.“As far as the network is concerned, lahat naman po kami, we all have different pro­jects. Hindi naman po ako favorite. It just so happens na after Move It started, heto naman ang Wattpad Presents episode ko.“Saka hindi naman po ako parating nabi­bigyan ng sunud-sunod na projects. The last series I did was Undercover and that was in 2013 pa. I did Jasmine but last year pa siya natapos.“The very last show I hosted was SPINNation and that ended last year din. Tapos ‘yung first Wattpad episode ko was also last year.“Kaya there are months din na wala akong ginagawa for TV5.”Naintriga pa tuloy ang kapwa Kapatid star niyang si Eula Caballero na nai-insecure raw sa kanya. Tumitindi nga raw ang ‘away’ ng dalawa sa TV5?“That is not true because Eula is a very good friend. We always keep in touch and share a lot of girl talk.“And knowing Eula, she is not an in­secure person. Ma­galing siyang artista and TV5 has big plans for her naman.“And kami ni Eula, we’re very, very okay. Friends kami niyan and she knows how much I love her being my friend,” nakangiting sabi pa ni Jasmine.At kahit na sobrang busy sa kanyang career si Jasmine, hindi naman niya pina­pabayaan ang kanyang pag-aaral. Currently enrolled si Jasmine sa online campus ng Swinburne University in Australia taking up Beha­vioural Science.
