Eastern Visayas is in for a rainy Wednesday on February 11 according to state weather bureau PAGASA.
The agency said in their Tuesday bulletin that the region may have light to moderate rains on this day.
Cagayan Valley and the provinces of Quezon and Camarines Norte can expect light rains brought by partly cloudy to cloudy skies.
Metro Manila and the rest of Luzon need only prepare for isolated light rains.
The rest of Visayas and the entire region of Mindanao meanwhile may have isolated rainshowers or thunderstorms.
Due to the Northeast Monsoon, moderate to strong winds will prevail in Luzon making ocean waters here moderate to rough.
The rest of the archipelago can expect light to moderate winds making sea waters calmer.
